# Patent Search Sites (CN/US + Global)

## China-focused
- CNIPA public service portal: https://ggfw.cnipa.gov.cn/
- CNIPA patent search & analysis system (PSS): https://pss-system.cponline.cnipa.gov.cn/
- CNIPA patent information entry: https://www.cnipa.gov.cn/col/col171/index.html
- Wanfang patent search: https://c.wanfangdata.com.cn/patent
- PatentStar: https://www.patentstar.com.cn/

## US-focused
- USPTO Patent Public Search: https://www.uspto.gov/patents/search/patent-public-search
- USPTO general patent search page: https://www.uspto.gov/patents/search
- PatentsView (US data/API): https://patentsview.org/
- Justia Patents: https://patents.justia.com/

## Cross-jurisdiction
- Google Patents: https://patents.google.com/
- WIPO PATENTSCOPE: https://patentscope.wipo.int/
- Espacenet (EPO): https://worldwide.espacenet.com/
- The Lens: https://www.lens.org/
